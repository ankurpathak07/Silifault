About SiliFault

Revolutionizing wafer defect detection with advanced AI technology

Our Mission

SiliFault is dedicated to transforming the semiconductor manufacturing industry by providing cutting-edge AI-powered solutions for wafer defect detection. Our mission is to help manufacturers improve yield rates, reduce production costs, and ensure the highest quality standards in their products.

Founded by a team of experts in computer vision, machine learning, and semiconductor manufacturing, SiliFault combines deep industry knowledge with state-of-the-art technology to deliver solutions that address real-world challenges in wafer inspection.

Our Technology

At the heart of SiliFault is our proprietary AI technology, which has been trained on millions of wafer images to detect and classify defects with unprecedented accuracy. Our system can identify various types of defects, including edge defects, scratches, donut defects, and random contamination.

The technology behind SiliFault includes:

Deep Learning

Advanced neural networks trained on extensive datasets to recognize patterns and anomalies in wafer images.

Computer Vision

Sophisticated image processing algorithms that analyze wafer surfaces at the microscopic level.

Real-Time Processing

Optimized for speed, our system can analyze wafer images in milliseconds, enabling inline inspection.
